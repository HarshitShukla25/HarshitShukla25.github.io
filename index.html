A Little About Us

"Help will be given to those who seek it."

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E Cell) is an institute body run by the students of IIT (BHU) Varanasi devoted to acting as a symbiotic link between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell and the existing start-up ecosystem as well acting as a hub where all start-ups can meet, collaborate and innovate. The organization functions with a vision to develop a dynamic hub committed to aiding an entrepreneur or entrepreneur-to-be in unwrapping her/his hidden potential and providing her/him with the guidance and resources if need be. We work towards the development of a start-up hub in Varanasi providing top-quality education and opportunities in the future.
