About Us

Get to know the E-Cell and the purposes it serves

The Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT(BHU) is institute run body run by the students of IIT(BHU) Varanasi devoted towards acting as a symbiotic link between entrepreneurs of E-Cell,IIT(BHU) and the existing startup ecosystem as well acting as a hub where all startups can meet, collaborate and innovate.

What is the Cell about?

The Entrepreneurship-Cell woks towards strengthening ties with the willing and talented entrepreneurs towards a development of startup hub in Varanasi providing numerous opportunities and top quality education in the future. The Entrepreneurship Cell as a whole will be responsible for the apposite interaction between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell,IIT(BHU) and the startup ecosystem.

On its mission towards building an entreprising India, Entrepreneurship Cell IIT BHU provides great opportunities for startups, colleges, alumni and corporates to get involved with us. The Structure of our E-Cell represents our concern over different aspects of our vision.

Meet The Team


The Core Team
It carries out day to day activities of the E-Cell as well as manage the Insitution's Entrepreneurship Hub for all the entrepreneurial activities. It is responsible for co-ordination between the various teams and arranging meetings. It mediates the interactions between the students and the successful entrepreneurs.
Strategic Relations team
It establishes the relations with external organizations and development and growth of the startups. Cementing long term partnerships with the companies for support and guidance to startups during all phases. Networking with venture capitalists and incubators for funding of startups.
Start-up Assistance Program Team
Discussing with the entrepreneurs the feasability of their ideas and scope of improvements. Mentorship at the time of product development, making them aware of the competition, and informing them of the mistakes their competitors have made in the past. Ensuring ideas of any aspiring student entrepreneur does not get stolen by a member of the E-cell and taking appropriate actions against a team member if found guilty of doing the same.
Events Team
Organizing the various flagship events of the E-Cell such as Google Startup Weekend, Hult Prize, E-Summit, Online B-Plan Contest.Conducting Workshops and seminars by eminent personalities in the field of entrepreneurship and business. Managing the logistics, operations and hospitality of these events.
Web and Branding team
It is responsible for development and maintenance of the official website of E-Cell. Managing the official social media accounts of the E-Cell and maintaining an interactive and far-reaching presence across all platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram etc. Providing the requisite technical platform and support for the online events conducted by the events team.
Research Innovation and Entrepreneurship Unit
The unit strives to enhance the outlook of students towards research fields. Successful research work can be converted into useful and commercially viable products and the unit would assist them in tranforming their research thesis into business models or patents. Precisely,it is a platform to blend pragmatic researcha and entrepreneurship.
