Internship Portal
Portal for internship/part-time roles in Stealth/Early Stage Startups for students.

Mentorship Program
Sophomores guiding the freshmen with their general entrepreneurship-related queries. Having discussions on different entrepreneurial topics and news to increase their engagement as well as awareness of the startup ecosystem.

Startup 101 Program
In this series of workshops followed by intergroup competition, we get to know about many tools and techniques essential to building a successful product and eventually a startup. We take a gamified approach of case competitions to imbibe various skills and bootstrap strategies. This program promotes a collaborative learning environment where freshers, as well as the mentor, gain knowledge from those around them.

Founders Speak
A webinar series from unicorn founders and serial entrepreneurs to share their journey into the startup ecosystem.

Know Your Department
Sessions for freshers on the research opportunities in their department. Preferably speakers will be dept. alumni pursuing PhD abroad.
